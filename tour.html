Project Overview.

Self initiated project to rebrand the 2013 Tour of Britain Cycle Race and to raise the profile of the sport within the UK. The logo for this campaign used the Union Jack to suggest the spokes of a bike wheel. I used the red white and blue to give the new brand a modern British feel I then applied this to other elements of the project including poster, apps and leaflet.
