Project Overview.

Workshop project set by Silo Design Studio to find novel ways to promote Falmouth as a tourist destination. My idea was to illustrate a 3D robotic seagull that would follow you around and pester you until you went to Falmouth. The illustrations could appear in a magazine, newspaper or be used to create a working model to promote the town.
